The Best Movies of All Time

aka Indi's Favorite Movies

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Makes me cry everytime at the end. Love the messages in it, between the mother/daughter/family dynamic and just feeling unaccomplished in life
Like the grass is greener on the other side.

Black Panther 1 + 2

Both movies are near and dear to my heart, especially the second after Chadwick's passing.
First movie was so important and amazing for black people like myself. We got to see ourselves as heroes and villains, dynamic not static. I cried.
Second movie was beautiful tribute to Chadwick and even expanded on what the first movie couldn't/didn't in terms of themes + the theme of grief and acceptance.
I also cried.

Any Studio Ghibli film

Honestly enough said. It's Studio Ghibli (minus Tales from Earthsea) and those movies are peak.
Will absolutely watch again, maybe even tonight or this weekend :D

Earth to Echo

Grew up with this movie, watched it as a kid. I honestly couldn't think of a third movie, but this one came to mind for some reason.
I remember wanting a friend group like the one in the movie, and wanting something like that to happen to me. It was a fun movie (to me) to watch.
And of course, I cried LOL.
